                   ▼
               🤖 LLM

Why Hybrid (BM25 + Vector)?

Query: "CORS error on /api/v2/auth SDLC-1042"

BM25 finds:   "SDLC-1042"  "CORS"  "/api/v2/auth"   ← exact keyword match
Vector finds: "header missing after nginx reload"    ← semantic meaning

Neither alone catches everything. Together they do.
